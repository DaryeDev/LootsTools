Introduction to Cards

Cards are the redeemable items of Streamloots, and we catch them to execute the Commands you, the streamer, want.

Commands are executed by two types of cards: Normal Cards, which execute a list of actions on a file; and Advanced Cards, which execute a Python script on redeem.

Continue on Normal Cards or Advanced Cards.
